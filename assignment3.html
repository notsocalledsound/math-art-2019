assignment3 : bees and pie

Today is all about circles and arcs, radians and pi.

Make your own version of the gif by "bees and bombs" that is linked below.

Use nested for loops and arcs to make each half circle.

You might also use a conditional statement : if(){} to add some interesting complexity to your sketch.

Make the idea your own by changing colors, spacing, rotation speed, overlap of circles.

Or, use the basic code structure (nested for loops) to do something completely different.
